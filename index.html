About VVT

MedAI ( Medical Artificial Intelligence ) is a Decentralized Science and AI Project (DeSci + AI) built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). MedAI aims to revolutionize healthcare accessibility by leveraging blockchain technology, AI, healthcare system. With MedAI, users can access telehealth services, wellness programs, and educational resources in the metaverse.

How to work

MedAI is a Utility-Meme and governance token within the healthcare ecosystem. MedAI can be used to pay for telehealth consultations, medical services, and wellness programs. Holders of MedAI can participate in decentralized decision-making and governance processes related to the future development of the MedAI Labs platform. MedAI NFTs can be earned as rewards for achieving health goals and completing health education challenges.
